Summary

Inception is a 2010 science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan. It follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who enters the dreams of others to steal secrets. He is offered a chance to have his past crimes erased if he can successfully plant an idea into someone's subconscious.
