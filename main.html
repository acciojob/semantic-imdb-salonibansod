Summary

Inception is a mind-bending thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. The story follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief, who specializes in stealing secrets from within the subconscious during the dream state. He is given a chance to erase his criminal record by performing an almost impossible task: inception, the planting of an idea into a person's subconscious.

- Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur
- Ellen Page as Ariadne
